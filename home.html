IRIS - Intelligent Resident Interaction System

Created by VisibleHand, LLC

VisibleHand is dedicated to improving patient behavioral health care through the use of digital technology. We believe caregivers have the power to positively influence their patient's quality of life and we strive to ensure all caregivers have innovative and expert technological tools at their fingertips. We care about the patient and the caregiver.

Demo

FEATURES

Proactive Behavioral Strategies
Real-time, resident-centered information on secure mobile devices allows care staff to respond appropriately and proactively to resident behavioral needs.
Continuous Training and Improvement
Supervisory staff are able to monitor results to discover expertise and identify needs for additional training.
Care plan optimization
Individualized patient care interventions are in the hands of frontline care givers.
Documentation Improvement
Caregivers are capable of identifying and documenting behaviors and interventions in real time producing significant improvements in frequency and accuracy of behavior tracking.
Medication Reduction
Reports and analysis tools allow you to track the effects of reducing or changing medication levels.  With iris,  accurate behavior documentation assists clinicians to make informed decisions regarding medication additions, reductions or changes.
Alerts, when you need them
Sign up for SMS or email alerts for facility-wide or resident-specific events.

Trend analysis charts help you understand how behavior is changing, and when behavior needs are greatest
